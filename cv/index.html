Project: Evaluating Policy Options for New Treatments Under Aug 2024 – 2026
Uncertain External Evidence

  • Designed a novel conceptual diagram to compare policy options on new treatment with external evidence, from never granted without a local trial, delayed access pending a local trial, immediate access without a local trial, and immediate access while awaiting a local trial.
  • Developed a partitioned survival model to estimate the monetary values from the combination of sintilimab and chemotherapy or the chemotherapy only for treating first-line locally advanced or metastatic nonsquamous non-small-cell lung cancer to illustrate the conceptual diagram.
  • Employed Python to compute the expected value of perfect information (EVPI), the expected value of sample information (EVSI), and the expected net benefit of sampling (ENBS) using a two-level double-loop Monte Carlo approach, comparing the expected monetary benefits of four policy options.
  • Published in Value in Health (2026).
